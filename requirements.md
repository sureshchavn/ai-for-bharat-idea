# MedConnect AI – Intelligent Medicine Availability & Smart Recommendation Platform

## 1. Introduction

MedConnect AI is a web-based platform designed to help users find the availability of medicines in nearby pharmacies and receive AI-powered recommendations for alternative generic medicines when stock is unavailable.

The system aims to reduce time, effort, and stress faced by patients while searching for medicines, especially during emergencies.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

In India, patients often:

- Visit multiple pharmacies to find prescribed medicines.
- Face stock shortages without knowing alternatives.
- Experience delays during emergencies.
- Lack awareness about generic substitutes.

There is no centralized intelligent system that provides real-time availability and smart medicine substitution.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Provide real-time medicine availability.
- Suggest AI-based alternative generic medicines.
- Handle misspelled medicine names using NLP.
- Reduce search time for patients.
- Support small pharmacies with digital visibility.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

1. User Registration and Login.
2. Search medicine by name.
3. Display nearby pharmacy availability.
4. Show stock status (Available / Out of Stock).
5. AI-based alternative recommendation.
6. Basic medicine information display.
7. Admin panel for pharmacy inventory update.

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

- System should respond within 2–3 seconds.
- Secure user data handling.
- Scalable architecture.
- User-friendly interface.
- Mobile-responsive design.

---

## 6. Target Users

- Patients
- Pharmacists
- Rural health workers
- Small medical store owners

---

## 7. Expected Impact

- Reduced medicine search time.
- Increased awareness of generic alternatives.
- Improved healthcare accessibility in semi-urban and rural areas.
- Digital empowerment of local pharmacies.
